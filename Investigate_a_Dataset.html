Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

we have csv file include dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment.

The file includes the following columns:

*PatientId__The patient's insurance number.

*AppointmentID____Medical examination reservation number.

*Gender__The gender of the patient (male or female).

*ScheduledDay_what day the patient set up their appointment.

*AppointmentDay___The date of the medical examination.

*Age_patient age.

*Neighborhood_indicates the location of the hospital.

*Scholarship__indicates whether or not the patient is enrolled in Brasilian welfare program Bolsa Família.

*Hipertension_The patient is suffering from high blood pressure.

*Diabetes_The patient suffers from Diabetes mellitus.

*Alcoholism___The patient is addicted to alcohol.

*Handcap__The patient has some type of disability.

*SMS_received_indicates that the patient received a message regarding the appointment date or not.

*No-show__indicates whether the patient attended the examination appointment or not.(it says ‘No’ if the patient showed up to their appointment, and ‘Yes’ if they did not show up).

What is the percentage of patients who did not attend?

Is the female more committed to appointment or male?

What is the percentage of patients who did not attend and have Hypertension?

def Mycountplot(df,x):
    df[x][Yes_show].value_counts().plot(kind='bar',alpha=0.7, color='green', label='Yes',fontsize=13)
    df[x][No_show].value_counts().plot(kind='bar',alpha=0.7, color='blue', label='No',fontsize=13)

    plt.ylabel('number of patients', fontsize=16)
